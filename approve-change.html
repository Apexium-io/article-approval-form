📝 Post Length Tips:
- LinkedIn: 100–300 words; clear, professional, well-formatted.
- X: Under 280 characters; short, sharp, and engaging.
- Facebook: 1–2 short paragraphs; keep it relatable.
- Instagram: 1–3 sentences or up to 200 words; hook readers early.

🏷️ Hashtag Guidelines:
- LinkedIn: Use 2–5 professional tags at the end.
- X: 1–3 short, trend-aware tags; keep it clean.
- Facebook: 1–3 tags max; optional and subtle.
- Instagram: 5–10 tags; blend niche + popular for reach.
